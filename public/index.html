About Us

Turning Kitchen Grease Into Green Energy — Since Day One

We're the folks who make collecting cooking oil effortless so you can focus on what matters: running a great kitchen.

GreaseCycle Pro was founded by kitchen veterans who got tired of the same old story: overflowing oil bins, nasty drain clogs, and surprise fines from the health department. We built a better way.

Today we partner with restaurants, hotels, food trucks, and commercial bakeries across the region. We provide free collection of waste cooking oil, handling the dirty work so our kitchen heroes can focus on their craft.
